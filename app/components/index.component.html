PHI KAPPA TAU

The Epsilon Chi chapter of Phi Kappa Tau is a fraternity at Virginia Tech, dedicated to learning, leading and serving. Founded in 2000, Epsilon Chi has built a reputation for superior gentlemen, leaders, and community members.
